## Multinational Corporation

Company that manufactures and markets products in many different countries and has multinational stock ownership and management

> not all global business are multinational,
> needs manufacturing capacity and physical presence

## Sovereign Wealth Funds (SWFs)

Funds controlled by governments holding investment stakes in foreign companies

The size of the funds and the fact that they are government-owned make some feat they might be used for:

- geopolitical objectives
- gaining control of strategic natural resources
- obtaining sensitive technologies
- undermining management of companies in which they invest
