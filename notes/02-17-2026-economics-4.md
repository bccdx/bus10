# Business Cycles

Periodic rises and falls that occur in economies over time

## 4 Phases of Long-Term Business Cycles

1. Economic Boom
2. Recession - 2+ consecutive quarters of decline in GDP
3. Depression - Severe recession
4. Recovery - When the economy stabilizes

# National Deficit, Debt, Surplus

## National Deficit

Amount of money the federal government spends beyond what it gathers in taxes

```
Revenue (Taxes, Fees, Property Rights) - Government Spending
= Deficit in Fiscal year (Oct 1 - Sept 30)
```

- Budget is made in Feb
- Submit to congress
- Congress passes

Deficit is paid for by borrowing, by issuing US treasury bonds to other countries

Recent bill added 1 trillion to deficit

## National Debt

Sum of government deficits over time

Total national debt is ~$39 trillion

## National Surplus

When the government takes in more than it spends
